Quality, Health, Safety, & Environment. Top priority.

At Greatship, safety takes precedence and is followed and implemented strictly and religiously. The term "safety" does not only entail safety of life and cargo but also encompasses protection of the environment.

Initiatives to implement and integrate strict safety measures for protection and preservation of environment have helped to uphold company's non-compromising attitude towards safety.

All activities related to the operation and management of vessels and rigs engaged in offshore services are certified for Environment Management system (ISO 14001:2004) and occupational Health and Safety Management system (OHSAS 18001:2007). The company is already certified for Quality Management System (IS09001:2000) and ISM.
